Foliar Mix 20-20-20

Balanced NPK foliar fertilizer for all crops

Foliar Mix 20-20-20 is a fully water-soluble fertilizer designed to provide balanced nutrition during all growth stages.

  • ✔ Enhances yield and quality
  • ✔ Suitable for all crops

Foliar application: 200–300 g / 100 L water
